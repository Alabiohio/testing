# implementation_plan.md

## Project Name

ccb.farms -- Catfish Sales & Training Website

## Project Overview

This project is a responsive business website for a catfish farming and
sales brand serving farmers and consumers. The platform will be deployed
on Vercel and connected to the domain ccb.farms.

## Main Navigation

Home \| Category \| Training \| Booked Order \| Contact Us

## Pages and Features

### Home

Hero section, About Us, Categories preview, Why Choose Us, How It Works,
Serving Farmers & Families, Call to Action.

### Category

Fingerlings, Juveniles, Fresh Table-Size Catfish, Smoked Catfish,
Broodstock.

### Training

Static training and guidance content.

### Booked Order

Full online order form including: - Live Catfish for Farming
(Fingerlings, Juveniles, Broodstock) - Catfish for Consumption (Fresh
and Smoked) - Delivery details - Customer information - Additional notes

### Contact Us

Phone, WhatsApp, location and contact form.

## Branding and UI

Shades of green, leaf green, deep green, reddish brown and black. Auto
dark mode using prefers-color-scheme.

## Technology Stack

Next.js, CSS, Vercel hosting.

## Form Handling

Client-side validation and submission through backend or email
integration.

## Deployment

Local setup, testing, domain connection to ccb.farms and final approval.

## Future Enhancements

Admin dashboard, payments, order tracking and training booking.
