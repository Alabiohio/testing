For a truly perfect fit (zero gaps)
If you want zero letterboxing at all breakpoints, you'd need to switch from object-contain to object-cover — but that crops. Since the ratios differ between mobile and desktop, a single image can't perfectly fill both with object-contain.

The 700 × 560px size will get you the closest fit with minimal-to-no visible bg-gray-50 gaps on desktop, and only thin side bars on mobile.
